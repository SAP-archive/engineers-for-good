Thanks to everyone that has been participating in the beta version of the community marketplace and sharing ideas and input! You can learn more about the marketplace commons here. The software team is working on the next phase of development:
 
  • Customized weekly newsletters based on location, language, and topic preferences to make it easier to find opportunities in your area or amplify your work
 
  • Improved marketplace navigation, search, and filters to make it easier to find physical products in your area, suppliers, services, places to visit, work opportunities, etc
 
  • Integration with ecommerce platforms like Shopify and Woocommerce so that community members with existing stores and marketplaces can sync their listings and direct traffic to their own sites
 
  • Opportunities to receive payments through the platform with a focus on serving community members without access to international payment gateways
 
  • Opportunities for networks to add their directory and marketplace to their own websites
 
Basic marketplace listings will continue to be free. If you are Good Market approved, you can add your products, services, places, events, and other opportunities to the community marketplace from your enterprise account page. The deadline for the next marketplace newsletter is September 12th.
