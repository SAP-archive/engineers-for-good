Go Local Campaign
Good Market community members across all parts of the country have come together to launch a Go Local campaign starting with this New Year season.
 
The goal is to celebrate local products that are high quality, ethically made, fairly priced, healthy, and environmentally responsible. "Going local" benefits our communities and reduces our impact on the planet. It's something everyone can do to help the country build back even better after a challenging year.
 
If you're interested in getting involved, send a WhatsApp message to +94770208642 with your name and Good Market approved brand name.
Good Market Shops
